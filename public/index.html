About Us

Taking inspiration from classical Abruzzo Cuisine. Cattedrale seeks to take the old world flavors reminiscent of L'Aquila and Sulmona and bring them to Providence, Rhode Island. Located in the northern section of DePasquale Plaza , Cattedrale resides in a ornate Italianate building historically inspired by the ninteenth century Providence Custom House. Rooted in tradition Cattedrale provides insight of the rustic life of Abruzzo to a modern audience.
